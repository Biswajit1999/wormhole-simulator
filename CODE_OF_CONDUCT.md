# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1.

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and
expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality,
personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include using
welcoming and inclusive language, respecting differing viewpoints, gracefully
accepting constructive criticism, and focusing on what is best for the community.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments,
personal or political attacks, publishing others' private information, and other
conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported
to the maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and
fairly. The full text of the Contributor Covenant 2.1 governs interpretation and
enforcement: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
